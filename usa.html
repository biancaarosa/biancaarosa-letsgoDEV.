EXCHANGE IN THE UNITED STATES: TOURISM AND STUDY TO GET TO KNOW THE COUNTRY IN A DIFFERENT WAY!

Doing an exchange in the United States is one of the best ways for you, in addition to acquiring fluency in the language, to enjoy the vast cultural program in the country, such as concerts, shows and world-renowned theater plays.

Annually, around 45 million tourists from all over the world visit the country, to get to know its modern metropolises, natural beauties and the famous American way of life up close.

The great territorial extension and geographic diversity offer the practice of the most varied activities and extreme sports, in addition to the beautiful landscapes. No matter what your goals, in addition to the many exchange options in the United States, you will return to Brazil with new cultural insights and much more confident with your English.

Studying a language course in the United States means having the opportunity to meet students from all over the world, participate in social and cultural activities, play sports and travel extensively with the Experimento Exchange Program in the United States.

We offer several exchange options such as Au Pair, Language Courses, High School, Vacation Program, Professional and University. Check out how each one works and sign up for the best one for you!

Technology: the United States is the 3rd most innovative country in the world.

According to the 2021 Global Innovation Index, the United States ranks third in innovation, demonstrating the country's resilience and concern for the topic even during the pandemic.
The 2021 index compared 132 economies and the US surpassed even countries like Germany, Japan, China and Australia. Brazil ranked 57th in the same global ranking and 4th in Latin America.
And the results could not be different: the USA is the birthplace of large technology companies (fintechs), startups and shopping centers.

The best English to study.

Polemic theme!! It's not that there really is one variation of the English language better than the other, okay? But American English is what we see most in movies, series, music… The United States dominates the internet and its languages, as well as the world of arts.

Looking at it this way, it makes sense that American English is the best to study, right? You will be learning from what is most heard and used around the world.

Also, studying in the USA you will be in contact with people from all over the world. That is, you will be practicing the language and putting your skills into practice with people with different accents and vocabularies. You can see that studying in the United States is a complete learning experience, right?
