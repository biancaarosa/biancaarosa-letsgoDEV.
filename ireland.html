EXCHANGE IN IRELAND: STUDY, WORK, ENTERTAINMENT, RICH CULTURE AND SPORTS

The exchange in Ireland is the ideal opportunity to improve your English and get to know one of the liveliest and most welcoming countries in Europe. Formed by the combination of the cultures of different peoples such as the Celts, who inhabited the island for thousands of years, Vikings, Normans and British, Ireland manages to unite natural beauties and man-made buildings, some recordings of the series Game Of Thrones are made there. , in breathtaking landscapes.

In addition to Gaelic, their native language, the Irish use English as their official language. Although the accent may cause some initial difficulties, the hospitality and friendliness of the locals make up for the language differences and communication flows very easily.

The Irish people are one of the most hospitable on the entire European continent. With little time in the country, exchange students already feel at home and start to live the Irish culture intensely. A good way to get to know the city where you will be studying abroad is walking, because most attractions are usually concentrated in a small area. Bicycles are also an excellent means of transport.

For fans of the sport, the mountains favor climbing and the beaches offer good waves for surfing and good diving spots that provide beautiful views of the seabed. The country also has disputed professional sports championships. Traditional football, rugby and cricket. Horse sports also stand out, mainly racing, polo and horseback riding.

For those who like excitement, studying in Ireland is a full plate: around 40% of the country's population is under 25 years old, that is, it is a young country, ideal for the student who wants to have fun. For that, there are several pubs and nightclubs that are not afraid to be eclectic: within the establishments, Celtic culture, Britpop and electronic music share space democratically.

One of Ireland's great differentials is the ease of obtaining a visa, which is requested directly upon arrival, at no cost and without bureaucracy. Upon arrival, the student will have 4 weeks to complete their registration with the immigration department.
